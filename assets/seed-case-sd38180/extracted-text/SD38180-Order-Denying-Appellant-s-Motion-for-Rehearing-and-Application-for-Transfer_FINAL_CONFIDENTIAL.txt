                                            ORDER

     Now on this day, the court takes up appellant’s “MOTION FOR RECONSIDERATION,
REQUEST FOR EN BANC REVIEW TRANSFER, AND PETITION FOR INJUNCTIVE RELIEF FOR
ONGOING VIOLATION OF FEDERAL RIGHTS UNDER 42 U.S.C. § 1983 AND THE EX PARTE
YOUNG DOCTRINE,” filed April 20, 2024. The court construes said filing as a motion for
rehearing and application for transfer. See Missouri Supreme Court Rules 83.02 and 84.17(a)(1).
Having fully considered the same, the court hereby denies the filing in its entirety. See Missouri
Supreme Court Rule 83.04. The court further takes up and denies any and all other requests for
relief by appellant that have not previously been ruled upon by the court.
